About Radha Meditech

Established in 2006, Radha Meditech is a trusted supplier of Medical and Surgical products across India and more than 50 countries worldwide.

Our portfolio caters to multi-speciality healthcare needs, offering products for General Surgery, Ophthalmology, Gynaecology, Laparoscopy, Urology, Cardiology, Neurology, ENT, Endoscopy, and Respiratory care.

We serve Government, Semi-Government, Private, and Not-for-Profit institutions, providing products certified with ISO, CE, SFDA, ISI, GMP, and WHO-GMP.
